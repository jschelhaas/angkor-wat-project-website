Context of the Site within the Khmer Empire

Angkor Wat fits into the major themes evident to the Khmer Empire. The site symbolizes cultural identity while also giving critical evidence of how the Khmer Empire used advanced technology to sustain a complex society. The integration of the hydraulic system to this religious monument reflects this. A technology that has obvious practical needs is also used to support spiritual needs, underscoring the importance of water management on general sustainability. This theme also connects to the empire’s political stability, as control over water resources reinforced the king’s authority. The application of a complex water management system coupled with a rich background in religion and culture as evident at Angkor Wat gives insight to the function and dominance of the entire empire.
